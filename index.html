How to play

GOAL: The objective when playing Mastermind is to guess the correct Hidden Colors in the correct order in eight or less guesses.

GAME START: The computer generates random colored pegs, which are hidden behind the white pegs on the top of the display. Be aware multiple pegs of the same color are possible.

ATTEMPTING A GUESS: Click on one of the colored pegs to the side of the grid. Your selected peg is placed on the board, starting at the bottom row and in column 1. Once you select all 4 pegs for your guess it will use up an attempt. Don't worry, before you complete an attempt you can undo your last peg if you made a mistake. After each row is complete, the game will provide feedback.

FEEDBACK PEGS:

  • If the guessed pegs are correct in both color and position, a black peg will be displayed on the board.
  • If a guessed peg is correct in just color but wrong in its position, a pink peg will be displayed on the board.
  • Feedback pegs fill from left to right, therefore they don't hint at the position of the peg color.
